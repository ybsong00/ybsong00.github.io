Biography

Yibing Song currently works for Tencent. He has obtained PhD/Mphil degrees from City University of Hong Kong in 2018/2014 and a bachelor degree from University of Science and Technology of China in 2011. During graduate study, he has interned in Adobe Research, San Jose and visited UC Merced. Yibing Song focuses on the new trends of computer vision including self-supervised representation learning and adversarial attacks. Meanwhile, he is still interested in traditional topics including motion and tracking, visual recognition and generation.
